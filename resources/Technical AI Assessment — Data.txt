NYC Taxi & Limousine Commission Trip Record Data
Dataset:
 https://www.nyc.gov/site/tlc/about/tlc-trip-record-data.page
You may use:
* Yellow taxi data only
* A subset of 1–3 months of data
* CSV or Parquet format
You are encouraged to limit the dataset size appropriately for local development and the allotted time.
Assignment
Using the assigned NYC Taxi Trips dataset, design and build a small analytics platform that demonstrates how you organize, transform, validate, and present data across modern data engineering layers.
Your solution should include:
* A clearly structured dbt project with bronze, silver, and gold models
* Data quality tests and documentation
* A Streamlit dashboard powered by your gold-layer models
The goal of this exercise is not simply to create charts, but to demonstrate your approach to building reliable, maintainable, and business-ready data systems.


Core Requirements
Visualizations
1. Trip Volume Over Time
   1. Allow filtering by day/week/hour
   2. Show trends or peaks in demand
   3. Clearly define the aggregation grain
2. Revenue by Pickup Zone
   1. Show top-performing pickup zones
   2. Include at least one metric:
      1. total revenue
      2. average fare
      3. trip count
   3. Support filtering or sorting
3. Payment Type Analysis
   1. Visualize how rider behavior differs across payment types.
4. Additional Visualization (Candidate Choice)
   1. Add one additional visualization or analytical feature that you believe would provide meaningful business value.
Expectations
We specifically want to understand your approach to organizing data across layers
You are encouraged to extend the system with additional functionality, features, and technical considerations you believe are necessary to make this a production-ready and polished product.
